\# 📞 FitNova AI Call Intelligence



An AI-powered sales call intelligence platform that analyzes advisor-customer conversations, evaluates advisor performance, detects potential issues, and generates actionable insights.



\---



\## 🚀 Overview



FitNova AI Call Intelligence is a Python-based application designed to help organizations analyze sales and customer service calls.



The platform processes audio recordings through an AI-powered pipeline:



```text

Audio Recording

&#x20;     ↓

Speech-to-Text Transcription

&#x20;     ↓

Speaker Labeling

&#x20;     ↓

Call Analysis

&#x20;     ↓

Performance Scoring

&#x20;     ↓

Actionable Insights
